"Coffee is beautiful and can be way more than just a beverage"

The journey of Personal Brew Coffee started back in 2023 after a roadtrip to Norway and a visit at Tim Wendelboe's Cafe in Oslo. Since then we roast coffee on a small drum roaster. The roaster is a Kaleido M1 sample roaster with batch size of up to 200g. As a result, Personal Brew Coffee offers bags where each bag is one individual roast batch.
