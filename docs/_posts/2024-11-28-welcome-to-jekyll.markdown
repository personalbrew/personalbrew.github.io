My name is Patrick and I am a coffee enthusiast. My love to coffee developed over time from the usual bean to cup machine, store bought beans towards a proper espresso machine and finally light roasted filter coffees.
There is a really nice online community for filter coffee. So why not build an in-person community as well?
I would like to offer you a very personal coffee experience at my place. The usual process is that we have a short call and talk about your preferences such that I can make the booked coffee experience as personal as possible to you.

At my place you can enjoy the most exquisite coffees from around the world.
Usually I store coffees from roasters such as
Tim Wendelboe, The Picky Chemist, Banibeans, etc.
